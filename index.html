How to Play

Move the player through the maze and push every crate onto a glowing target.

Rules

  • You can move up, down, left, and right.
  • You can push one crate at a time.
  • Crates cannot be pulled.
  • Walls block both the player and crates.
  • The level is cleared when all crates are on targets.

Controls

  • Keyboard: Arrow Keys or W A S D
  • Restart Level: R
  • Undo Move: U
  • Next Level: N
  • Back / Close: Esc
  • Mobile: Touch on-screen buttons
